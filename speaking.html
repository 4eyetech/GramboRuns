Speaking

Let's share a story together

I speak about the things that move me: the discipline of long-distance running, the unexpected joy of starting later in life, the grief and gratitude of legacy, and the quiet power of showing up for yourself one day at a time.

My audiences include running clubs, senior communities, wellness organizations, corporate teams, and anyone who believes that the best stories are the ones that make people feel less alone.

Topics I can bring to your event

  • Running the Abbott Six Star majors — lessons from six cities and 157.2 miles
  • Starting after 50: fitness, mindset, and the courage to begin again
  • Bear Hugs from Mom: legacy, love, and carrying memory forward
  • Encouragement as a practice: how to support the people around you
