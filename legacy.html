Legacy

Bear Hugs from Mom

Some legacies are written in stone. Others are wrapped in arms, tucked into goodbyes, and carried forward in the way we show up for one another. Bear Hugs from Mom is the story of a love that did not end — it simply changed shape.

My mother gave hugs that made you feel held, seen, and safe. She did not run marathons, but she knew something every marathoner learns: the hardest moments are survived not by speed, but by presence. Her love taught me that strength can be gentle, and that encouragement is one of the most powerful forces on earth.

Carrying it forward

This part of the site is a living tribute. Here, I share the lessons she left behind, the stories that still make me smile, and the quiet ways I try to pass her warmth on to others — especially to older adults who may be wondering if their best chapters are behind them.

If you are here because you miss someone, because you are searching for hope, or because you need a reminder that love outlasts loss, please know: you are welcome here.
